Leche Flan

Leche flan is a rich, creamy, and velvety Filipino custard dessert made from egg yolks, condensed milk, and evaporated milk, topped with a layer of amber-colored caramel syrup. It is traditionally steamed in an oval tin pan called a llanera, resulting in a dense, smooth, and melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Ingredients

Caramel:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup water

Custard:

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 12 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Using all the eggs, separate the yolk from the egg white as only egg yolks will be used
  2. Place the egg yolks in a big bowl then beat them using a fork or an egg beater
  3. Add the condensed milk and mix thoroughly
  4. Pour-in the fresh milk and Vanilla. Mix well
  5. Put the mold (llanera) on top of the stove and heat using low fire
  6. Combine sugar and water on the mold over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil without stirring to avoid the mixture crystallizing.
  7. Spread the caramel (liquid sugar) evenly on the flat side of the mold
  8. Cover the top of the mold using an Aluminum foil
  9. Steam the mold with egg and milk mixture for 30 to 35 minutes.
  10. After steaming, let the temperature cool down then refrigerate
  11. Serve for dessert. Share and Enjoy!
